BITW

Bitwise 10 Crypto Index Fund

A secure way to get diversified exposure to bitcoin and leading cryptocurrencies. The Fund seeks to track an Index comprised of the 10 most highly valued cryptocurrencies, screened & monitored for certain risks, weighted by market capitalization, and rebalanced monthly. The fund provides the security and simplicity of a traditional investment vehicle, with shares tradable in brokerage accounts using ticker “BITW”.
